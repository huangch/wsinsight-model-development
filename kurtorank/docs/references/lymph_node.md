# Lymph Node Reference Notes

## Stromal framework

- Rodda et al., Cell 2018, single-cell atlas of murine lymph node stroma describing fibroblastic reticular cell subsets expressing CCL19/21, IL7, CXCL12, PDPN, COL1/3/5/6, and integrin/VCAM programs mirrored in the expanded FRC panel.
- Malhotra et al., Journal of Experimental Medicine 2012, transcriptional profiling of FRCs versus follicular dendritic cells highlighting chemokine gradients (CXCL9/10/11) and ECM-remodeling genes (MMP2/14, TIMP1/2, TNC, FBLN1/2) used here.

## Immune parenchyma

- Smoligovets & Dustin, Annual Review of Immunology 2014, review of T-zone organization and T-cell dynamics supporting CCR7/SELL/KLF2/S1PR signaling, co-stimulatory molecules, and effector modules (PRF1, GZMK) adopted for paracortical T cells.
- Reizis, Nature Reviews Immunology 2019, plasmacytoid dendritic cell overview (IL3RA, CLEC4C, TCF4, IRF7/8, interferon-stimulated genes) guiding the lymph node pDC gene list.
- Bernink et al., Nature Immunology 2013, characterization of human tonsillar/lymph-node ILC3 populations expressing RORC, IL23R, KIT, NK receptors, and IL22/IL17 cytokines.

## Sinus macrophage specializations

- Gray & Cyster, Nature Reviews Immunology 2012, outlines subcapsular versus medullary sinus macrophage niches, including CD169/SIGLEC1, complement receptors, and interferon response genes.
- Bruce et al., eLife 2018, single-cell transcriptomics of human lymph node macrophages that delineate STAB1/2, TREM2, VSIG4, LYVE1, and scavenger/lysosomal modules used to expand medullary panels.

## Lymphoid malignancies

- Steidl et al., New England Journal of Medicine 2010, gene-expression landscape of Hodgkin Reed-Sternberg cells with STAT/JAK, NF-κB, immune checkpoint (PD-L1/PD-L2), and chemokine signatures incorporated into the HRS set.
- Rimsza et al., Blood 2008, mantle/follicular lymphoma transcriptional profiles citing BCL2, BCL6, EZH2, CARD11-MALT1-BCL10, and proliferation modules reflected in the follicular lymphoma markers.
- Kiel et al., Blood 2012, genomic features of nodal marginal zone lymphoma (NOTCH2, KLF2, MYD88, TNFAIP3, BIRC3) plus chronic BCR signaling pathways informing the marginal zone panel.
