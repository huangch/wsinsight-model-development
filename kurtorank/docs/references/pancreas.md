# Pancreas Reference Notes

## Endocrine program

- Baron et al., Cell Systems 2016, single-cell RNA-seq resource for human pancreatic endocrine lineages (beta, alpha, delta, PP) — supplies canonical transcription factors (PDX1, NKX6-1, ARX, MAFA/MAFB) and ion channel composition supporting ≥50-marker panels.
- Segerstolpe et al., Cell Metabolism 2016, complementary single-cell atlas confirming hormone processing genes (PCSK1/2, CHGA/B, SLC30A8) plus vesicle machinery (STX1A, SNAP25, SYT4/7).
- Bastidas-Ponce et al., Development 2019, developmental roadmap describing INSM1/RFX6/NEUROG3 hierarchies used to extend endocrine progenitor and PP/gamma lists.

## Exocrine, centroacinar, and tuft cells

- Muraro et al., Cell Systems 2016, provides enzyme-rich acinar signatures (PRSS/CPA/CELA families) and ductal markers (KRT8/18/19, CFTR, SLC transporters) that anchor expanded gene sets.
- Tosti et al., Nature Communications 2021, profiles human pancreatic ductal/tumor interface and captures ADM, centroacinar, and tuft-like states with SOX9/AXIN2/POU2F3 programs.
- Haber et al., Nature 2017, intestinal tuft roadmap leveraged for shared chemosensory modules (POU2F3, GNAT3, TRPM5, IL17RB) detectable in pancreatic tuft cells within inflamed ducts.

## Stellate and stromal activation

- Apte et al., Gut 2013, classic review of pancreatic stellate biology supporting ECM/retinoid genes (LRAT, COL1A1/2, LUM, LOX) for quiescent and activated signatures.
- Hosein et al., Cancer Discovery 2019, defines inflammatory vs myofibroblastic CAF programs (CXCL12/IL6 vs ACTA2/TAGLN) that informed separate stellate states.

## Malignancies

- Bailey et al., Nature 2016, molecular subtypes of PDAC covering KRAS/TP53/SMAD4 lesions plus EMT/ECM modules (COL11A1, ITGA6/ITGB4, LAMC2).
- Scarpa et al., Nature 2017, whole-genome sequencing of pancreatic neuroendocrine tumors providing MEN1/DAXX/ATRX/TSC2/MAPK alterations and neuroendocrine markers (CHGA, SYP).
- Tang et al., Mod Pathology 2016, transcriptomic profiling of solid pseudopapillary neoplasms highlighting CTNNB1/LEF1 axis and hormone receptor expression (PGR/ESR1).
- Hijioka et al., Cancer Science 2016, characterizes mixed neuroendocrine-nonneuroendocrine neoplasms (MiNEN) including shared KRAS/TP53 lesions with neuroendocrine markers.
- Hoadley et al., Cell 2018 (Pan-Cancer Atlas), leveraged for broad carcinoma programs used in acinar cell carcinoma and pancreatoblastoma panels (MYC, BIRC5, TOP2A, ECM remodeling genes).
