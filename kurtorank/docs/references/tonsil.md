# Tonsil Reference Notes

## Epithelial compartments

- Suo et al., Nature 2018, human cell landscape dataset that profiles tonsillar stratified squamous epithelium and supports keratin/envelope genes (KRT4/13/6, SPRR family, IVL, FLG) plus junctional components (CLDN1/4, COL17A1, LAMC2).
- Kanaya et al., Nature 2012, demonstrates RANKL-driven crypt M-cell differentiation with SPIB, SOX8/9, GP2, ITGAX/ITGB7, and TNFRSF11A/11 signaling used for microfold marker expansion.

## Germinal center network

- Victora & Nussenzweig, Annual Review of Immunology 2012, canonical germinal center B-cell program (BCL6, AICDA, CXCR4/5, POU2AF1, RGS13) and proliferative regulators (MKI67, TOP2A, MCMs).
- Crotty, Nature Reviews Immunology 2019, comprehensive review of T follicular helper differentiation highlighting CXCR5, PDCD1, ICOS, IL21, BATF, TOX/TOX2, and SLAM signaling genes.
- Nutt et al., Science 2015, outlines plasma-cell terminal differentiation circuitry (PRDM1, IRF4, XBP1, TNFRSF17, secretory pathway chaperones) referenced for long-lived plasma cell markers.

## Broader immune subsets

- Sallusto et al., Nature Reviews Immunology 2004, defines central/memory T-cell hallmarks (IL7R, CCR7, SELL, BACH2/FOXO1) with effector-associated transcripts (KLRG1, IFNG) used for tonsillar memory panels.
- Reizis, Nature Reviews Immunology 2019, plasmacytoid dendritic cell review covering lineage-defining genes (IL3RA, CLEC4C, TCF4, IRF7/8) and interferon-stimulated modules (ISG15, MX1, OAS family).
- Bernink et al., Nature Immunology 2013, characterizes human tonsillar ILC3 clones retaining RORC, AHR, IL22/IL17A/F, IL23R, KIT, NCR2/3, and CCR6 signatures.

## Stromal and vascular niche

- Girard et al., Nature Reviews Immunology 2009, detailed description of high endothelial venules expressing PNAd machinery (ST3GAL6, CHST4, B3GNT3, FUT7) and vascular adhesion molecules (SELE/L/P, ICAM1, VCAM1, CCL21) deployed here.
- Heesters et al., Nature Reviews Immunology 2014, follicular dendritic cell microenvironment references for CXCL13, complement receptors, adhesion molecules, and stromal matrix cues (VCAM1, ICAM1).

## Lymphoid malignancies

- Rimsza et al., Blood 2008, gene-expression profiling of mantle cell lymphoma highlighting CCND1, SOX11, BCR/TCR signaling modules (SYK, BTK, CARD11/MALT1) and proliferation markers (MKI67, AURKA/B, PLK1).
- Rosenwald et al., Cancer Cell 2002, diffuse large B-cell lymphoma molecular subtypes capturing MYC/BCL2/BCL6 programs, NF-κB pathway components, and PI3K/AKT survival genes used for malignant tonsillar panels.
