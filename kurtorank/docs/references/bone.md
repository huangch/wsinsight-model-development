# Bone Reference Notes

## Skeletal stroma and cartilage lineages

- Tikhonova et al., *Nature* 2019, Baryawno et al., *Cell* 2019, and Wolock et al., *Cell Reports* 2019 provide single-cell atlases for osteoblasts, bone-lining cells, and mesenchymal stromal progenitors, covering canonical matrix genes (COL1A1/2, ALPL, RUNX2) and niche cytokines.
- Youlten et al., *Nat Commun* 2021 plus Schaffler & Kennedy, *Bone* 2012 resolve osteocyte maturation cascades (SOST, MEPE, DMP1) that informed osteocyte marker selection.
- Ji et al., *Nat Commun* 2019 and Lefebvre & Bhattaram, *CSH Perspect Med* 2018 describe hypertrophic chondrocyte programs (COL10A1, MMP13, RUNX3) bridging growth plate to ossified bone.
- Craft et al., *Cell Reports* 2019 along with Hardouin & Pino, *Bone* 2016 chart marrow adipocyte transcription (ADIPOQ, LPL, PPARG) retained for adipocytic stromal rows.

## Mesenchymal, vascular, and progenitor niches

- Baryawno et al., *Cell* 2019 and Wolock et al., *Cell Reports* 2019 define MSC subsets expressing CXCL12, PDGFRA, and LEPR that support hematopoiesis.
- Xie et al., *Nat Commun* 2020 and Tikhonova et al., *Nature* 2019 profile sinusoidal vs arteriolar endothelial cells (KDR, EMCN, AQP1) plus perivascular cues (SEMA3G, ESM1).
- Laurenti & Göttgens, *Nat Rev Immunol* 2018 synthesize hematopoietic stem/progenitor hierarchies (KIT, CD34, GATA2, MECOM) incorporated into the HSPC panel.

## Osteo-immune crosstalk

- Novack & Teitelbaum, *Annu Rev Pathol* 2008 and Guerrini et al., *Nat Rev Rheumatol* 2018 delineate osteoclast differentiation (RANK, CTSK, ACP5, NFATC1), ensuring accurate resorptive signatures.
- Batoon et al., *Bone Research* 2021 and Albers et al., *Nat Commun* 2022 describe osteal macrophages and marrow-resident TAMs (CSF1R, TREM2, VSIG4, LYVE1) supporting myeloid marker curation.

## Primary bone tumors

- Sayles et al., *Nat Commun* 2019 and Perry et al., *Nat Commun* 2020 provide genomic and transcriptomic profiles of osteosarcoma, capturing RUNX2, SP7, COL1A1, and proliferative modules used for osteoblastic tumor cells.
- Nacev et al., *Cancer Discov* 2020 plus Niinimäki et al., *Nat Commun* 2022 anchor chondrosarcoma markers (SOX9, COL2A1, ACAN, IDH1/2 mutation programs).
- Tirode et al., *Cancer Cell* 2014 and Riggi et al., *Nat Rev Cancer* 2021 summarize Ewing sarcoma EWSR1-FLI1 targets (NKX2-2, NR0B1, STAG2) applied to the small-round-cell list.
- Chen et al., *Nat Commun* 2020 and Guenther et al., *Cancer Cell* 2017 delineate giant cell tumor stromal populations (H3F3A mutants, RANKL-high), guiding osteoclastogenic stromal panels.
- Tarpey et al., *Nat Commun* 2017 plus Saito et al., *Cancer Discov* 2020 capture chordoma signatures (TBXT, KRT18/19, brachyury) while Clarke et al., *Mod Pathol* 2020 and Suda et al., *Clin Cancer Res* 2021 inform adamantinoma keratin/EMT programs.

## Hematologic and metastatic disease

- Manier et al., *Nat Commun* 2018 and Ledergor et al., *Nat Med* 2018 profile multiple myeloma plasma cells (SDC1, XBP1, IRF4, TNFRSF17) across disease stages.
- Li et al., *Nature* 2022 and Goswami et al., *Cancer Discov* 2023 dissect pediatric and adult ALL blasts, supporting the lymphoid leukemic marker panel.
- Zhang et al., *Nature* 2013 with Esposito et al., *Cancer Cell* 2019 define breast cancer bone metastasis programs (PTHLH, CXCR4, ITGA5), while Quigley et al., *Nat Genet* 2018 and Morrissey et al., *Nature* 2020 capture prostate metastatic signatures (AR, KLK3, HOXB13, integrin/EMT cues).
- Liu et al., *Nat Commun* 2020 and Chevalier et al., *Cancer Cell* 2020 outline lung adenocarcinoma bone colonization modules (MMP9, EPCAM, CXCL8) retained for metastatic epithelial rows.
